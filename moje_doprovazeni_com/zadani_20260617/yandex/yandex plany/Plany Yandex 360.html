Stále máte otázky?

Za tarif ve webové verzi služby si můžete zaplatit z počítače nebo telefonu.
Press "Cancel plan" in your account and confirm the action. Your plan will remain active until the end of the paid period. If you purchased the plan in the Yandex Mail or Yandex Disk app, you can cancel it only in the same app.
You can gift 3, 6, or 12 months of Yandex 360 Personal or Family with 200 GB or 1 TB of storage. Select a certificate, enter the recipient's email address, write a message to go along with your gift, and complete the purchase. The certificate will be sent to the specified address.
To save all photos and videos from your phone to Yandex Disk, enable autouploads and turn on the "Upload to unlimited storage" option. Learn more in Help. If you want to import files from another cloud storage to Yandex Disk, download them to your computer and upload to the cloud using the Yandex Disk computer application.
Set up a mail importer from your previous mailbox to Yandex Mail. This way, you can move all your previous messages and forward the new ones. If you have a mailbox at your own domain, you can activate Yandex 360 for Business and delegate the domain to Yandex servers.
You can access your files on any platform. You can access Yandex Disk in the browser from your computer or mobile phone. You can also manage files via the mobile app or the computer application.
Yandex isn't responsible for the files you upload to Yandex Disk, but it may block files if they violate the Yandex Disk Terms of Use or have been reported by another user.
After the subscription expires, all its paid features will become unavailable. The paid storage space will also become unavailable. Because of this, you may run out of space on Yandex Disk. When there's no more free space on Yandex Disk, the access to it is limited. If you don't fix the situation, your Yandex Disk will be completely blocked after 44 days.
An exclusive email address lets you use any address available in the .ru domain zone, such as alex@design-kitchen.ru. You can use this email address in addition to your @yandex.ru address to log in to Yandex Mail and send or receive emails. This option is only available for personal mailboxes. If you have a business and want custom email addresses on your own domain, get Yandex 360 for Business.
Your plan is renewed automatically. At the end of each period, you'll be charged for the next one. Make sure you have enough money on your card.
After an unsuccessful debit attempt, your Yandex 360 plan will remain active for some time, and attempts will repeat. If no successful payment is made, the Yandex 360 plan will be frozen and its benefits will become temporarily unavailable. This may cause you to go over your Yandex Disk storage limit. If your plan gets frozen, you'll still have some time to top up your balance and unfreeze the plan to keep its benefits. The service fee will be debited automatically, and you'll be able to continue using Yandex 360 services without restrictions again. If no successful payment is made, the plan will be deactivated completely.
If you already have a Personal or Family plan, it will stay active. You'll be charged for both plans, and the Yandex Disk space will be combined.
Make sure your card is valid, there's enough money on it, and the bank doesn't block automatic payments. Learn more about why a payment may not go through in Help. You can also pay for the plan manually. In your account, click "Pay now", select a card, and confirm the payment.
The file upload limit is the monthly volume of files and documents that you can upload to your Yandex Disk. For the Personal and Family plans, the limit is the volume of your Disk multiplied by 2. For the Pro plan, the limit is increased — 3 volumes of your Disk.
